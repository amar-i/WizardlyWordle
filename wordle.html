Wizardly Wordle Instructions

Your mission is to uncover the hidden word from the world of Harry Potter in six attempts.

  1. Type your guesses and cast your spell by clicking "Enter"
  2. Colors reveal the magic:
    • Green: Correct and in the right position.
    • Gold: Correct but in the wrong position.
    • Dark: Incorrect.
  3. Decipher the magical word using the clues.

Warnings:

  • Stay vigilant, the dark arts may confuse you.
  • Incorrect guesses cast shadows, choose wisely.
  • The true word is a secret known only to the magical realm.

Triumph:

Succeed in unveiling the hidden word to become the Wordle Wizard!

Successfully unveil the hidden word within six attempts, and you shall be hailed as the Wordle Wizard! Your name will echo through the enchanted halls of Hogwarts.
